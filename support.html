❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I send money? ▾
Hand your cash to Murphy (your agent), then open the app and go to Send Money. Enter the amount, select your recipient, upload a photo of your cash, and confirm. Murphy will process your transfer.
How long does a transfer take? ▾
Most transfers are completed within 24 hours after Murphy confirms receipt of your cash. You'll see the status update in your Transactions page.
What is the service fee? ▾
The service fee is set by your agent and shown clearly before you confirm your transfer. It's automatically calculated when you enter your send amount.
My balance is zero. What do I do? ▾
Your balance is topped up by your agent Murphy after you hand over your cash. Contact Murphy directly and your balance will be updated in the app.
Can I cancel a transfer? ▾
Contact Murphy immediately if you need to cancel. Transfers that are already Processing or Completed cannot be reversed. Reach out as early as possible.
I forgot my password. What do I do? ▾
Go to the login page and tap "Forgot password?" Enter your email address and we'll send you a reset link immediately.
